Sloth

Contrary to their reputation, sloths only sleep about 10 hours a day.

  • Scientific name: Bradypus variegatus
  • Average Speed: 4 metres per minute
  • Average Lifespan: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: South and Central America

Sloths have very low metabolic rates (less than half of that expected for a mammal of their size) and low body temperatures when active 30 to 34°C. Sloths are heterothermic, meaning their body temperature may vary according to the environment, normally ranging from 25 to 35°C, but able to drop to as low as 20°C.
